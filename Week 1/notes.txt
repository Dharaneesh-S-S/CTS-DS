SRP: One class, one responsibility.
ISP: Clients shouldn't depend on methods they don't use.
OCP: Open for extension, closed for modification.
LSP: Child class should replace parent without breaking behavior.
DIP: Depend on abstractions, not concretions.
DI: Technique to inject dependencies.
Singleton: One instance globally.
Factory: Creates one product type.
Abstract Factory: Creates families of related objects.
Builder: Handles complex object construction.
Adapter: Makes incompatible interfaces work together.
Facade: Simplifies a complex subsystem.
Observer: One-to-many notification mechanism.
Strategy: Switch algorithms at runtime.
Constructor Injection: Immutable, testable, and recommended by Spring.
